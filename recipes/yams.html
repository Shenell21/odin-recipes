Southern Yams

A Thanksgiving traditional dish with a bourbon twist!

This dish is a sweet treat to have during the holiday season! Be it Thanksgiving or Christmas, you can't go wrong with a side of delciious southern yams. Nutmeg, cinnamon, and your favorite bourbon all comingle together to prodcue a heavenly aroma and amazing taste. This is a side your family and friends will request again and again every year!

Ingredients

  • 1 long strip orange peel, sliced
  • 1/3 cup bourbon
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg
  • 1 3-4 inch cinnamon stick
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 3 cups water
  • 4 large sweet potatoes

Steps

  1. Wash and dry sweet potatoes and bake at 400 degrees Farenheit for one hour, or until soft to touch. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly then remove skin from potatoes.
  2. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees Farenheit. While the potatoes are baking, combine remaining ingredients in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30 to 45 minutes until sauce is slightly thickened.
  3. Slice the potatoes 1/2 inch thick into a medium casserole dish. Pour syrup over them and return to the oven and bake for approximately 30 minutes.
